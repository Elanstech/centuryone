Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our property management services in NYC

Property management fees in NYC typically range from 6% to 12% of monthly collected rent, depending on property type, number of units, and services needed. Century One Management offers competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. We provide a free consultation where we'll build a custom quote based on your property and goals.

We serve all five boroughs of New York City with a strong presence in Queens (Rego Park, Jamaica, Hollis, Woodside, Forest Hills, Astoria) and Manhattan. Our portfolio includes residential, commercial, and luxury properties. We're actively expanding and welcome inquiries from property owners across the metro area.

Our full-service management includes tenant screening and placement, rent collection, 24/7 maintenance coordination, lease administration, financial reporting, property marketing, legal compliance, vendor management, and investment consulting. Every plan is customized — you choose the level of service that fits your needs.

We use Door Loop, a leading property management platform. Tenants can pay rent online, submit and track maintenance requests, access lease documents, and communicate with our team through a secure portal. Property owners also get their own portal with real-time financial reports and property updates.

Yes. We manage commercial properties including office buildings, retail spaces, and mixed-use developments. Our commercial services include lease administration, CAM reconciliation, facility management, vendor coordination, and detailed financial reporting tailored to commercial property owners and investors.

We can typically onboard a new property within 1-2 weeks. This includes a property assessment, tenant communication, lease review, vendor setup, and portal activation. For urgent situations — like a vacancy that needs to be filled or a management company transition — we can expedite the process.

We provide 24/7 emergency support. Tenants can reach us at any time for urgent issues like burst pipes, heating failures, or security concerns. Our vetted vendor network allows us to dispatch qualified professionals quickly, and you'll be notified of any emergency and the steps taken to resolve it.

Our comprehensive tenant screening includes credit checks, background checks, employment and income verification, rental history verification, and personal references. We follow all Fair Housing laws and select tenants who meet our strict criteria — reducing the risk of late payments, property damage, and evictions.
