Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve properties throughout New York City and the surrounding metropolitan area, with a focus on Queens, Manhattan, and Brooklyn.

How quickly can you respond to maintenance requests?

We offer 24/7 emergency response for urgent issues. Non-emergency maintenance requests are typically addressed within 24-48 hours.

What are your management fees?

Our management fees vary based on property type, size, and specific service needs. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote.

Do you handle tenant screening?

Yes, we provide comprehensive tenant screening including credit checks, employment verification, rental history, and background checks.
