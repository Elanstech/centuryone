Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our property management services

What sets Century One Management apart from other property management companies?

Century One Management combines decades of industry experience with cutting-edge technology and a commitment to personalized service. We manage each property as if it were our own, with meticulous attention to detail, transparent communication, and a focus on maximizing returns while preserving your property's value. Our team of dedicated professionals has extensive knowledge of the local market and provides tailored solutions to meet your specific needs.

What is your fee structure for property management services?

Our management fees vary based on the specific services required, property type, size, and location. We offer customized management packages tailored to each property's unique needs. During our initial consultation, we will perform a thorough assessment of your property and provide a transparent fee structure that aligns with the level of service your investment requires. Contact us for a personalized quote and management plan.

How do you handle property maintenance and emergency issues?

We maintain a 24/7 emergency response system for urgent property issues. Our dedicated maintenance team coordinates with a network of vetted, licensed contractors to address maintenance needs promptly and efficiently. For routine maintenance, we implement preventative programs to identify and resolve potential issues before they become costly problems. Property owners can set maintenance spending thresholds, and all maintenance activities are documented and reported through our portal for complete transparency.

How do you screen potential tenants?

Our rigorous tenant screening process includes comprehensive background checks, credit evaluations, income verification, employment history, rental history, and reference checks. For luxury properties, we employ enhanced verification processes. Our goal is to identify reliable, qualified tenants who will treat your property with respect, pay rent on time, and maintain long-term tenancies. This thorough screening significantly reduces the risk of problematic tenants and potential evictions.

What type of financial reporting do property owners receive?

Property owners receive detailed monthly financial statements that include income and expense reports, payment histories, maintenance records, and occupancy updates. Our Door Loop platform provides 24/7 access to real-time financial information through a secure owner portal. We also provide year-end financial summaries and assistance with tax preparation documentation. Our reporting is designed to give you complete visibility into your property's financial performance.

How do you market properties to minimize vacancies?

We implement a comprehensive marketing strategy that includes professional photography, virtual tours when appropriate, compelling property descriptions, and targeted advertising across multiple platforms. Our marketing team utilizes both traditional and digital channels to reach qualified prospects, including MLS listings, property websites, social media, and our extensive network of real estate professionals. We also strategically time lease renewals and begin marketing well before vacancies occur to minimize downtime between tenants.

Have more questions? We're here to help.
